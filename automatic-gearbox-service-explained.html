Why Gearbox Service Prices Vary

Many gearbox service kits only include around 7 litres of oil. This is usually enough for a standard drain and refill procedure only.

During a dynamic gearbox exchange, additional oil is often required because old oil remains trapped throughout the torque converter, oil cooler, transmission circuits and valve body.

Transmission Standard Service Dynamic Exchange
BMW ZF 6HP Approx. 7L Approx. 10–11L
BMW ZF 8HP Approx. 7L Approx. 11–13L
Mercedes 9G-Tronic Varies Higher oil quantity required
Audi DSG / S-Tronic Varies Higher oil quantity may be required
A lower advertised price does not always mean the same level of gearbox servicing is being carried out.
