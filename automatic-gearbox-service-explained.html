Gearbox Service Explained

Automatic gearbox oil helps with lubrication, cooling, hydraulic pressure and smooth gear selection. Over time the oil can become dark, contaminated and less effective. This page shows why a professional gearbox service is different from a basic oil change.

What Happens During The Service?

We use specialist gearbox service equipment to exchange old oil with fresh oil that meets the correct specification. This can remove more contaminated fluid than a basic drain and refill, especially from cooler lines, internal channels and the torque converter area.

1. Oil Condition Check

Old oil condition is inspected for darkness, contamination and signs of overheating.

2. Dynamic Oil Exchange

The service machine helps exchange old gearbox oil with fresh correct-specification fluid.

3. Final Checks

Oil level, operation and any visible concerns are checked before the vehicle is returned.
