Risk Of Ignoring The Problem

Incorrect wheel alignment can cause uneven tyre wear, pulling to one side, poor handling and increased tyre replacement costs. It can also affect comfort and road safety.

What This Service Helps With

When To Book

  • Car pulling to one side
  • Uneven tyre wear
  • Steering wheel off centre
  • After suspension or tyre work
