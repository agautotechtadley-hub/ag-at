Risk Of Ignoring The Problem

Ignoring DPF problems can lead to a blocked filter, limp mode, failed regenerations, increased fuel use and expensive repairs. Replacing parts without diagnosis often does not solve the root cause.

What This Service Helps With

  • DPF pressure readings checked
  • Regeneration behaviour reviewed
  • Related systems inspected
  • Root-cause approach before replacing parts

When To Book

  • DPF warning light
  • Vehicle in limp mode
  • Frequent regeneration issues
  • Poor fuel economy or loss of power
