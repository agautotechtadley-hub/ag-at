Carbon Build-Up & Walnut Blasting

Intake valve cleaning for direct injection petrol engines suffering from carbon deposits. Our technicians use professional diagnostic equipment to identify the cause accurately and recommend the correct repair.

Risk Of Ignoring The Problem

Carbon deposits continue to restrict airflow, reducing performance and efficiency. In some cases they can contribute to running issues and misfires.

Common Symptoms

  • Rough idle
  • Loss of power
  • Reduced fuel economy
  • Engine hesitation
  • Misfires

How We Can Help

  • Intake carbon build-up inspection
  • Walnut blasting service
  • Before and after advice
  • Performance and running issue assessment

When To Book

  • The engine idles roughly
  • The car hesitates when accelerating
  • Fuel economy has dropped
  • A direct injection petrol engine has high mileage
