DPF Pressure Gun Cleaning Tadley

DPF Pressure Gun Cleaning is a specialist DPF cleaning service carried out with a pressurised cleaning gun system. This method is designed to clean the DPF in place, without removing the DPF from the vehicle, when diagnosis confirms the service is suitable.

Risk Of Ignoring The Problem

Ignoring DPF restriction can lead to failed regeneration, limp mode, warning lights, increased fuel consumption and expensive repairs. Cleaning without first checking the cause may not solve the issue, so the correct process is important.

What This Service Helps With

  • No DPF removal required in suitable cases
  • Specialist pressure gun cleaning method
  • Targets soot and contamination inside the filter
  • Helps restore flow when the DPF is suitable for cleaning

When To Book

  • DPF warning light
  • Restricted or blocked DPF symptoms
  • Regeneration problems
  • Vehicle going into limp mode
